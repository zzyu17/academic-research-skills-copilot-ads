# Quality Assurance Feedback Loops in Higher Education

## Abstract

Feedback loops are central to institutional accreditation. We synthesize two
strands of prior work and outline a measurement lens for assurance of learning.

**Keywords:** quality assurance, accreditation, feedback loops
